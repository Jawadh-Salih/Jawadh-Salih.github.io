Finding patterns was the work of Mathematicians in previous century. But with the advancement of usage in mathematics Computers do the work for us now. There are many techniques in Computer Science to find patterns of real life scenarios. Data is highly subjected to this because we can find many patterns in data where we did it from our childhood. We started recognizing patterns starting with Arithmetic series to complex series using mathematics. But now this has been even harder. Now we are finding patterns of real life data to earn profit for an organization and we are going to find patterns of life which is impossible for now as well as hereafter. Image Processing, Computer Vision, Digital Signal Processing, Stock Market Data Analytics etc. are done by finding abnormal or common behaviours in the relevant data. For these to be done, there are dozens of Techniques such as Regression, Neural Networks,Dynamic Time Warping, Frequent Item-set Mining, Association Rule Mining etc. And now we have got a better discovery by Mathematicians which is called as “Shapelets”. As the name suggests we are going to find similar shapes in graphs (functions or curves) . Especially in Time Series Classification problems we can now use this technique to derive useful classes from that Time Series. Time Series is a function which is depended on time.
